PRODUCT CONTEXT — Spotify Chart Intelligence

Spotify Chart Intelligence is a lightweight product that helps music teams instantly understand what is changing on Spotify charts every day.

The product tracks chart movements across key regions and delivers clear, actionable insights without requiring manual effort.

CORE FUNCTIONALITY:

* Tracks Spotify charts across major regions (Global, India, US, UK, etc.)
* Identifies:

  * New songs entering charts (primary signal)
  * Fast-rising tracks (momentum)
  * Region-wise trends
* Sends a daily summary at 10 AM IST
* Allows on-demand exploration via a Telegram bot

USER EXPERIENCE:

Users do not need to check dashboards or analyze data.

They simply:

* Receive a daily summary of key changes
* Can interact with the bot to explore specific regions

CORE PROBLEM:

Music teams currently:

* Manually check Spotify charts across regions
* Spend time identifying what changed
* Lack a clear, structured view of daily movement

This leads to:

* Missed early signals
* Inefficient workflows
* Slow decision-making

CORE VALUE:

The product tells you:

"What changed in Spotify charts today."

This includes:

* Which new songs entered charts
* Which songs are rising fast
* What is trending across regions

PRODUCT POSITIONING:

This is NOT a dashboard or analytics tool.

It is:

* A signal layer
* Focused on speed and clarity
* Designed for quick decision-making

KEY DIFFERENTIATION:

* No dashboards
* No complexity
* Instant insights
* Delivered via Telegram

TARGET USERS:

* Music labels
* Marketing agencies
* Artist managers
* Growth teams

PRIMARY USE CASE:

Daily awareness of chart changes.

SECONDARY USE CASE:

Quick exploration of specific regions on demand.

GOAL OF THE DECK:

* Clearly communicate the value
* Show how simple and useful the product is
* Make users want to try it immediately

TONE:

* Sharp
* Minimal
* Clear
* Confident
